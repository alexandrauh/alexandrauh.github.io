ABOUT

THE STORY

Born 1994 in the north parts of Sweden, grew up with a tech loving father, animal loving mother and a game loving brother.

The first few years I was a shy little girl who loved horses and played violin. But then came Nintendo 64 and the gaming door opened. Though before calling myself a gamer I had to go through some phases: Ditching the violin for guitar, abandoning mainstream music for rock and emo music, which of course included an emokid-phase. Somewhere in the middle of that a boyfriend came in to the picture. Of course it was this first boyfriend who introduced me to World of Warcraft, and I became a true PC gamer. This was around 2008. I was also a guitarist in a basement punk-rock band at the time.

Later, in 2010, it was time to start high school. I decided to leave this small town I grew up in and move to a bigger one and study media, graphic design. Given a new laptop and a bit too much freetime I started raiding in WoW. As well as discovering other online games like Minecraft and Diablo III.

Two years later one of my classmates discovered Twitch, he suggested that I would start streaming there. With a newfound love for Twitch I started streaming with the goal to have fun and grow my community around the stream. In August 2014 I got my partnership with Twitch. This was one of the happier moments of my life.

Today I'm working part-time at an advertising agency, making websites, layouts and I'm also a photographer. Streaming is mainly a hobby, but also a part-time thing. Currently I'm streaming a mix of IRL streams and casual gaming.
